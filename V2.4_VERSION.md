# QuestFit v2.4 - The Rhythm System Update

This update introduces the **Rhythm System**, allowing QuestFit to sync with Samsung Health to track your real-world rhythm and convert it into powerful game mechanics.

## New Features
- **Samsung Health Integration**: Securely connect to Health Connect to pull sleep and workout data directly into the game. All data is processed locally on-device.
- **Rhythm Screen**: A new dashboard replacing the Settings tab on the main navigation bar. It shows your sleep breakdown, Aether earnings, and current rest buff status.
- **Aether Currency (🔮)**: Burn active calories during your workouts to earn Aether (50 calories = 1 Aether). You can earn up to 30 Aether per day.
- **Rest Buff System**: Good sleep is now rewarded in-game! Get at least 5 hours of sleep to earn the "Well-Rested" buff, which provides up to a +20% multiplier to LP and Damage. High quality sleep (Deep + REM) increases the buff multiplier.
- **Workout Intensity Bonuses**: Workouts are analyzed using heart rate zones. Pushing yourself into higher zones (Aerobic, Threshold, Max Effort) will provide future damage multipliers.
- **Consistency Tracking**: The Rhythm Engine tracks when you work out. Maintaining a consistent daily schedule grants bonus Gold!

## Technical Changes
- Updated Android Gradle Plugin (AGP) to 8.9.1 to support the latest `androidx.health.connect` libraries.
- Upgraded the `health` package to version 13.3.1.
- Advanced Database Migration (Schema V6) for managing Aether, sleep minutes, and buff multipliers.
- Fixed a layout overflow issue on the home screen top bar by implementing horizontal scrolling.

## Bug Fixes
- Fixed an app crash (Null check operator on null value) that occurred during database initialization and data migration.
