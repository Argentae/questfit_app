# QuestFit v2.3.0 — The Bounty & Expedition Update

**Release Date:** May 20, 2026

---

## ⚡ Highlights

### 🎯 The Bounty Board
The core quest system has been completely redesigned into the **Bounty Board**, offering a more gamified and engaging daily combat loop.

- **Active Hunts**: Face off against 12 unique elemental enemies (Goblins, Drakes, Golems, Serpents, etc.).
- **Elemental Combat**: Enemies have elemental types (Fire, Water, Earth, Wind, Shadow, Light) and HP values.
- **Dynamic Routines**: Build your daily workout routine to deal damage to the active bounty.
- **Daily Drafts**: A new bounty is generated every day you log in.
- **Victory Rewards**: Defeating a bounty grants massive gold, LP bonuses, and potential egg drops.

### 📚 The Grimoire (Exercise Library)
A massive upgrade to exercise selection!

- **800+ Exercises**: Fully seeded local database with over 800 categorized exercises.
- **Advanced Filtering**: Filter by Category (Strength, Cardio, Plyometrics, etc.), Level (Beginner, Intermediate, Expert), and Equipment.
- **Exercise Details**: Comprehensive instructions, primary/secondary muscle targets, and force/mechanic details for every exercise.
- **Full-Screen Picker**: Seamlessly build your routine by adding exercises directly from the Grimoire to your Active Hunt.

### 🥾 Expedition & Companions
Turn your daily steps into game progression!

- **Step Tracking Engine**: Daily step goals synced with the game economy.
- **Momentum Buff**: Hitting your daily step goal activates a Momentum Buff (+10% LP & Gold) for the next day.
- **Milestone Trail**: Claim rewards (Gold, LP, Eggs) at 2,000-step milestones throughout the day.
- **Egg Hatchery**: Incubate eggs using your real-world steps.
- **Companion Collection**: Hatch eggs to unlock permanent Companions (Pets) that provide passive buffs (e.g., +5% LP gain).

---

## 🔧 Technical Implementations

- **Navigation Architecture**: Fixed complex `ShellRoute` navigation issues using root navigators for full-screen dialogs and pickers.
- **Database Architecture**: Implemented robust Drift database tables for `ExerciseDb`, `BountyDb`, and `EnemyDb`.
- **Initialization Engine**: Built a robust `AppInitProvider` that seeds the exercise catalog, checks daily resets, and generates bounties securely on app launch without UI blocking.
- **State Management**: Migrated complex multi-provider states to Riverpod's latest patterns, utilizing `AsyncValue` and `addPostFrameCallback` for safe UI updates.
